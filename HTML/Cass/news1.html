    About

    Every year TEXAS lives up to the reputation that everything is BIGGER. Our event in Dallas continues to be one of our benchmark events in the U.S. and it is one you don't want to miss. One of our biggest collection of vendors comes together to provide the largest collection of shoes in the world and the best part is...they are all available to purchase. Bring your collection to the Trading Pit or set yourself up as a vendor for a full day of buying, selling and trading. The LEGIT booth is always open for business to make sure you are buying 100% authentic items and it is where you can learn what is real vs fake in sneakers. Check out the Sneaker ConVersations stage where you can access knowledge from some of the best, giveaways, and more. Get your tickets early to stay tuned on what is going down at this one.
